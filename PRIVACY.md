# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 30, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ThreadMail ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our Discord bot ("Bot"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information Provided by Discord
When you authorize the Bot, we receive the following information from Discord:
- Discord User ID
- Username and discriminator
- Guild/Server membership information
- OAuth2 access and refresh tokens

### 2.2 Information Generated by Bot Usage
- Ticket creation data (timestamps, case numbers)
- Thread IDs and channel information
- Guild/server settings and configurations
- Staff role assignments
- Custom channel settings

### 2.3 Information from Interactions
- Messages sent through tickets
- Command usage data
- Error logs and debugging information

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Core Functionality
- To provide ticket creation and management services
- To facilitate communication between users and server staff
- To maintain ticket history and status
- To send notifications and updates

### 3.2 Service Improvement
- To analyze usage patterns and improve functionality
- To debug and fix issues
- To provide customer support

### 3.3 Legal Compliance
- To comply with Discord's Terms of Service
- To enforce our Terms of Service
- To respond to legal requests when required by law

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 With Discord
We integrate with Discord's API and may share information as required for the Bot's functionality. We comply with Discord's Developer Terms of Service.

### 4.2 With Server Administrators
Ticket content and user information may be visible to server administrators and staff members with appropriate permissions within your Discord server.

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or government request.

### 4.4 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Data Storage
- User data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest
- Data is retained only as long as necessary for service provision

### 5.2 Data Retention
- Active user data is retained while the account is authorized
- Ticket data may be retained for audit and support purposes
- Data is deleted when accounts are deauthorized or upon request

### 5.3 Security Measures
- All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- Access to user data is restricted to authorized systems
- Regular security audits and updates are performed

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Control
- You can view and manage your authorized applications in Discord settings
- You can revoke the Bot's access at any time through Discord
- You can request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)

### 6.2 Data Portability
- You can export your ticket history through Discord
- Server administrators can access server-specific data

### 6.3 Opt-out
- You can stop using the Bot at any time
- Revoking authorization will stop data collection

## 7. Cookies and Tracking

The Bot does not use cookies or tracking technologies. All functionality is handled through Discord's OAuth2 system.

## 8. Third-Party Services

### 8.1 Discord Integration
- We use Discord's API for bot functionality
- Discord's Privacy Policy applies to data handled by their platform
- We are not responsible for Discord's data practices

### 8.2 Hosting and Infrastructure
- Data may be stored on third-party hosting providers
- All providers are selected for their security and compliance standards

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

## 10. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes through Discord announcements or other appropriate channels.

## 12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through:
- Discord support channels
- GitHub repository issues
- Email (if provided in the future)

## 13. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR and other regional regulations where applicable.
